Ankara

Ankara is the capital city of Turkey. It is the most populous city in the Anatolia, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the Sıhhıye, Ankara has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Ottomans, who named it Ankura.
